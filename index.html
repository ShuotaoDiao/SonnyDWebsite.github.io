Here's an overview of my research.

On the Interplay Between Stochastic Programming and Non-parametric Statistics

We developed distribution-free algorithms for predictive stochastic programming in the presence of streaming data to allow decisions under uncertainty to adapt to the knowledge of leading indicators. Link to the paper.

A Reliability Theory of Compromise Decisions for Large-Scale Stochastic Programs

We propose a unifying mathematical framework to support the reliability analysis of stochastic programming solutions resulting from the “compromise decision” process. Link to the paper.

Building Optimization Models for Mitigating Pandemic Surges and More

We are developing SEIR compartmental methods and stagedalert systems with wastewater signals to support wastewater surveillance project from the Illinois Department of Public Health. Link to the slides
